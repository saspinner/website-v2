My friends run an internet-famous dance club that doesn't exist. It began in 2022- they graduated,
moved to beautiful San Mateo California, and wanted an easy way for folks to visit. Instead of
sending out their new address, they registered a location on Google Maps, 'SMPGHQ' (bonus points if
you can guess the acronym). Now, anyone who wants to visit just needs to type those 6 letters and
they are in business.
